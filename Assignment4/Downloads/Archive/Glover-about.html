About

Cozy World is dedicated to delivering an exclusive collection of the most relevant brands and styles to a community of inspired people. Created in North Carolina in March 2017.


Website purpose

Our enduring purpose, that guides everything we do, is to inspire the dream of a better life through authenticity and timeless style. In the not too distant future, we hope to expand our collection to more than just shirts, such as creating different styles of hats, pants, and accessories. We have even started talking with some local businesses about renting a building, so that we may have a physical location that people come and shop at.

About the client

The creator of Cozy World is Aldo Mendoza-Santos and he currently attends UNCC as a junior. He came up with this idea when he was still in high school in Gastonia, NC and his purpose was for him to be able to create his own brand in hopes of addding something new to fashion.

Who website is for

This clothing line was created because we thought that it would be cool for people of all ages to be able to wear clothes that are comfortable and gender neutral.
